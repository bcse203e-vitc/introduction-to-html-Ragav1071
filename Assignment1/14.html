College Terms - Definition List

Semester
A half-year term in a college or university, typically lasting 5-6 months.
Credit
A unit that gives weight to the value, level, or time requirements of an academic course.
Internship
A short-term work experience offered by organizations for students to gain real-world exposure.
Syllabus
An outline of the subjects in a course of study or teaching.
CGPA
Cumulative Grade Point Average - an average of grade points earned across all semesters.
